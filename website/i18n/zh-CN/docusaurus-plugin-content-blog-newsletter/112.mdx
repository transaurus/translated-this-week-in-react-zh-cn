## React相关

[**语句与表达式**](https://www.joshwcomeau.com/javascript/statements-vs-expressions/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Josh Comeau正在筹备他的React新课程。他建议回归JavaScript基础，从而更好地理解JSX的工作原理——它只能处理表达式。关于这个话题，我期待[do-expressions](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-do-expressions?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)提案能取得进展😇。

[**如何在Storybook中构建关联组件**](https://storybook.js.org/blog/how-to-build-connected-components-in-storybook/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Storybook官方文章详细讲解了装饰器的各种用法：添加布局、全局Provider、模拟Redux store或请求...这能帮助你在Storybook中添加原本因上下文依赖而无法渲染的"关联"组件。

[**如何估算NPM包市场份额（以及Redux与其他库的对比）**](https://blog.isquaredsoftware.com/2022/07/npm-package-market-share-estimates/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Mark Erikson（Redux）解析了他分析npm包使用趋势的方法，基于各种可靠性不等的工具。下载量数据常与CI构建相关。他以React状态管理器为例，现在估计Redux/React使用率为33%（此前为45-50%）。

[**React中的无动作与无状态Reducer**](https://julesblom.com/writing/actionless-and-stateless-reducers?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

useReducer() 并非仅适用于复杂场景：它同样可以用于非常简单的状态机，比如将布尔值从 false 切换为 true。
