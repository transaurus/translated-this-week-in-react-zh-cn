## React技术

[**语句与表达式**](https://www.joshwcomeau.com/javascript/statements-vs-expressions/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Josh Comeau正在筹备他的React新课程。他建议先回归JavaScript基础，从而更好地理解JSX的工作原理——因为JSX只能处理表达式。同主题下，我期待[do-expressions提案](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-do-expressions?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)能有所进展😇。

[**在Storybook中构建关联组件**](https://storybook.js.org/blog/how-to-build-connected-components-in-storybook/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

这篇Storybook官方文章详细讲解了装饰器的各种用法：添加布局、全局Provider、模拟Redux store或请求...它能帮助你在Storybook中渲染那些因上下文依赖而无法单独展示的"关联"组件。

[**如何评估NPM包市场份额（兼谈Redux与其他库的使用对比）**](https://blog.isquaredsoftware.com/2022/07/npm-package-market-share-estimates/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Redux作者Mark Erikson解析了他分析npm包使用趋势的方法，这些工具的可信度各有不同。下载数据常与CI构建相关。他以React状态管理库为例，最新评估Redux在React项目中的使用率为33%（此前为45-50%）。

[**React中的无动作与无状态Reducer**](https://julesblom.com/writing/actionless-and-stateless-reducers?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

useReducer() 并非仅适用于复杂场景：它同样可用于非常简单的状态机逻辑，比如切换布尔值从 false 到 true。
