## React核心

[**Reassure**](https://github.com/callstack/reassure?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Reassure是全新的React性能测试库。此前已有介绍，现由Callstack开源。它能在检测到性能退化时（如组件渲染变慢或更频繁）在PR中生成报告。目前仅支持React-Native，但Web支持即将到来。

[**如何实现多人协作光标动画**](https://liveblocks.io/blog/how-to-animate-multiplayer-cursors?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

这篇精彩的交互式文章探讨了跨浏览器实时同步鼠标光标动画的实现。问题比表面更复杂，每种方案都配有React实现并呈现不同取舍：CSS过渡、弹簧动画、样条曲线...

[**Next.js中间件使用指南**](https://www.ctnicholas.dev/articles/how-to-use-nextjs-middleware?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

同作者的交互式文章，详解多种中间件应用场景。此前已发布，现根据Next.js 12.2正式版的破坏性变更进行了更新。

[**Meteor十年历程**](https://meteor10.sachagreif.com/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Sacha Grief（《Discover Meteor》合著者，JS/CSS现状调查发起人）回顾他与这个框架的十年渊源。Meteor虽热度减退但仍被使用，且深刻影响了前端生态。

[**React元素、子组件、父组件与重渲染之谜**](https://www.developerway.com/posts/react-elements-children-parents?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Nadia 重点解析了 React 中某些可能令人意外甚至反直觉的行为，并详细说明了在各种边界情况下父组件重渲染会触发子组件重渲染的具体场景。

[**Storybook 社区精选案例第二期**](https://storybook.js.org/blog/community-showcase-2/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Storybook 社区最新动态汇总：组件百科全书、Figma 插件、故事资源管理器，以及各类插件——变体管理、CSS 变量、Recoil 状态集成等。

[**React 状态管理的新浪潮**](https://frontendmastery.com/posts/the-new-wave-of-react-state-management/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

文章系统梳理了状态管理库需要解决的核心问题，并对主流方案进行横向对比。包含 Redux 时代的前后技术回顾。内容全面但篇幅较长，阅读门槛较高。

## React-Native

[**FlashList - 高性能React Native列表组件**](https://shopify.github.io/flash-list/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Shopify刚刚发布了FlashList，作为FlatList的替代方案，旨在解决滚动时的性能问题并避免显示空单元格。其目标是即使在低端Android设备上也能保持60fps，同时不增加实现的复杂性。该库会复用已渲染的单元格：您需要调整React `key`的使用方式以充分发挥其优势。

[**React-Native-Compat-Table**](https://retyui.github.io/react-native-compat-table/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

全面列出了每个React-Native运行时（JSC、V8、Android等）的API支持情况。作者还解释了[为什么React Native推荐TS配置库使用"es2017"](https://dev.to/retyui/why-react-native-typescript-config-recommends-to-use-a-five-years-old-standard-lib-es2017-3bjk?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)。
