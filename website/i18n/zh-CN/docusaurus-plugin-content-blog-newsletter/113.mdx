## React核心

[**源码解读：React**](https://alexkondov.com/readint-source-code-react/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Alex通过React应用挂载的切入点，逐层剖析React源码并注解。揭示了原型链运用、JSX转换机制，以及（平台无关的）协调器与DOM渲染器交互等技术细节。

[**我们能否承认React Hooks是个错误设计？**](https://medium.com/codex/can-we-all-just-admit-react-hooks-were-a-bad-idea-c48120c5188d?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Amy认为React Hooks违背了SOLID架构原则。笔者虽不完全认同，但承认网络上的示例多为简化版，未必体现最佳实践。

[**在React中实践SOLID原则**](https://konstantinlebedev.com/solid-in-react/?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

几乎是对上文观点的直接回应，尽管此处对SOLID原则的应用讨论更为全面（涵盖组件和钩子）。注：不妨大胆使用React上下文实现依赖注入。

## React-Native

[**Hermes成为默认引擎**](https://reactnative.dev/blog/2022/07/08/hermes-as-the-default?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Hermes JS引擎是JSC/V8的替代方案，通过预编译字节码可加速应用启动。随着React-Native 0.70（现已发布[候选版本](https://github.com/facebook/react-native/releases/tag/v0.70.0-rc.0?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)）的推出，Hermes已默认启用。Michael分享了开源应用Mattermost的多项基准测试。Hermes分发模式已调整以避免ABI兼容性问题。iOS平台新增了缺失的Intl API支持。即将支持BigInt和WeakRef。

[**在Airbnb React Native时代的工作经历如何教会我理解web3**](https://medium.com/@devinabbott/what-working-at-airbnb-during-its-react-native-era-taught-me-about-web3-6d34d855d892?utm_campaign=This%20Week%20In%20React&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)

Devin回顾了Airbnb使用React-Native的历史及其最终放弃的复杂决策过程，并与web3的发展进行了有趣类比。长期来看，技术潜力终将超越早期采用者的阵痛。
